## Full Description

**Your Personal Radio Experience**

Global Radio brings you curated audio content in your language, tailored to your interests. Whether you want to start your day with astrology predictions, catch up on news, or explore spirituality, we have something for everyone.

**🎧 Features:**

• **10 Indian Languages** - Hindi, English, Bengali, Tamil, Telugu, Marathi, Gujarati, Kannada, Malayalam, and Urdu

• **Multiple Interests** - Astrology, News, Spirituality, Entertainment, Health, Sports, Technology and more

• **Daily Updates** - Fresh content every morning based on your preferences

• **Background Play** - Listen while you work, commute, or relax

• **Voice Selection** - Choose from multiple AI voices that sound natural

• **Sleep Timer** - Fall asleep to your favorite content

• **Playback Speed** - Speed up or slow down to match your pace

• **Offline Mode** - Download content to listen without internet (Premium)

• **Ad-Free Experience** - Subscribe to Premium for uninterrupted listening

**🌟 Why Global Radio?**

• Simple, clean interface designed for everyone
• No complicated setup - just pick your language and interests
• Works on slow connections and offline
• Privacy-focused - we only collect what's necessary
• Made in India for Indian audiences

**📱 Premium Benefits:**

• No ads
• Unlimited downloads
• Higher quality audio
• Early access to new content
• Priority support

Download now and start your personalized audio journey!
