# Privacy Policy — Global Radio

**Effective Date:** January 1, 2025  
**Last Updated:** January 1, 2025

Global Radio ("we," "us," or "our") operates the Global Radio mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our App.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide
- **Account Information:** If you sign in with Google or Apple, we receive your display name and email address. We do not store passwords.
- **Language Preferences:** Your selected languages for content playback.
- **Interest Selections:** Topics you choose to follow (e.g., astrology, devotion, kids stories).

### 1.2 Automatically Collected Information
- **Device Information:** Device model, OS version, app version for debugging purposes.
- **Usage Analytics:** Anonymous playback statistics (which content is played, listening duration) to improve recommendations.
- **Push Notification Tokens:** If you enable notifications, we store your device token to send daily updates.

### 1.3 Information We Do NOT Collect
- Precise location data
- Contacts or call logs
- Microphone or camera access
- Financial or payment information (app is free)

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:
- Deliver personalized audio content in your selected languages
- Send daily astrology updates via push notifications (if enabled)
- Improve content recommendations based on listening patterns
- Debug technical issues and improve app stability
- Comply with legal obligations

---

## 3. Data Storage & Security

- **Local Storage:** Preferences and listening history are stored locally on your device using encrypted SQLite.
- **Cloud Storage:** Account information (if signed in) is stored securely in Firebase Authentication (Google Cloud).
- **No Third-Party Sharing:** We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

---

## 4. Push Notifications

- Push notifications are opt-in. You can enable or disable them at any time in app settings.
- We use Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) to deliver notifications.
- Topic subscriptions (e.g., "daily_astrology") are managed on your device.

---

## 5. Children's Privacy

Global Radio includes content suitable for children (kids stories). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent and believe your child has provided information, please contact us.

---

## 6. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:
- Access the personal data we hold about you
- Request correction of inaccurate data
- Request deletion of your data
- Opt out of push notifications
- Withdraw consent for data processing

To exercise these rights, contact us at: **privacy@globalradio.app**

---

## 7. Data Retention

- **Account Data:** Retained while your account is active. Deleted within 30 days of account deletion request.
- **Analytics Data:** Aggregated and anonymized; retained indefinitely for service improvement.
- **Push Tokens:** Deleted when you disable notifications or uninstall the app.

---

## 8. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:
- **Firebase Authentication** (Google) — for sign-in
- **Firebase Cloud Messaging** (Google) — for push notifications
- **Cloudflare R2** — for content delivery (CDN)

These services have their own privacy policies.
